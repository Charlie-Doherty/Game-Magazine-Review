Aj prosser

Super Mario Odyssey is a game that someone can play for hours on end without even feeling like time has passed, so many scenes will give you child-like flashbacks, remembering older games like 64 and galaxy, while still being completely original. While playing, no matter what age you will feel like a child playing this game, from its excellent narration to its stellar gameplay. This game had me in a trance from start to finish.
		
Pros- Comedic story, easily replayable, Many unlockable cosmetics, Fluid movement, Incredible music.
		
Cons- the game can feel too easy at times, Difficulty spikes can be annoying but nothing too hard, motion controls.

Saven Patel

I will say this now, I love this game. The game itself is very different from it's predecessors, being more open world it's still packed with things to do while having it's own unique challenges and secrets in each world. I have played the game three times, hundred percented the game the third time and have beaten the two extra world and unlike other games that make me overwhelmed with the things that I need to do Odyssey doesn't, the way the game feels makes you determined and confident that you will be able to do it. The game is very nostalgic for me, it is like playing Mario as a child for the first time, the feeling of happiness soared higher when I saw levels that were just like the 2-D platformers I played as a child, when I played them for the first time and knew I would love this game.

Thank you, Nintendo and anyone who helped work on the game and I am looking forward to your next Mario game.
